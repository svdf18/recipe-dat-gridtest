- That curry -

This delectable curry recipe is sure to be a hit with all who try it! The flavorful sauce is created by combining an array of aromatic spices (such as cumin, coriander, garam masala, and turmeric) with creamy coconut milk and rich tomato paste. The end result is an explosion of flavor that will tantalize your taste buds. Serve the curry over cooked basmati rice or with warm naan bread for a complete meal. To add a bit of extra flair, top with freshly chopped cilantro for a pop of flavor and a vibrant color. This dish is sure to become a family favorite and is the perfect way to liven up any dinner table. Bon appetit!

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add curry powder, cumin, coriander and cayenne pepper and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add coconut milk, tomato paste, water, sugar, salt and pepper and bring to a simmer.
  6. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Add peas and cilantro and cook for 2 minutes.
  8. Serve over cooked rice or with naan bread.
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Cooked rice or naan bread, for serving
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
